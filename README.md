## About Me

Hi, I'm **Gopal Luhar**, an **Operations & BPM Specialist** with **5+ years of experience** supporting international clients across business process management, CRM operations, back-office workflows, lead generation, documentation, and customer support.

I am now building my path into **Data Analytics** by combining real operations experience with practical tools like **Excel**, **Power BI**, **SQL**, and dashboard storytelling. I like working with messy business information, organizing it clearly, and turning it into reports that help people understand what is happening and what to do next.
